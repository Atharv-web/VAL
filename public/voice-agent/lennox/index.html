What components should be in my HVAC system? ⌄
A complete HVAC system typically includes an air conditioner or heat pump, a furnace or air handler, an indoor coil, and a thermostat. The Ultimate Comfort System brings together the best components from the Dave Lennox Signature® Collection for a fully integrated, high-efficiency system.
What reasons are there to replace an entire system instead of just a broken component? ⌄
Replacing an entire system ensures all components are matched for peak efficiency, reduces the risk of future breakdowns, and may qualify you for energy rebates. Mismatched components can reduce efficiency and void warranties.
How do I experience the benefits of an Ultimate Comfort HVAC system in my home? ⌄
Start by finding a certified Lennox dealer near you. They'll perform a free in-home assessment, recommend the right system for your home's size and needs, and handle professional installation to ensure optimal performance.
